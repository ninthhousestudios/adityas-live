--- Sun

The Sun represents the soul, the core identity, and the vital creative power that animates our existence. It acts as the king of the planetary cabinet, reflecting our authority, leadership, and the divine spark within.

--- Moon

The Moon governs the mind, emotions, and the nurturing capacity that allows us to connect with others. It reflects our internal comfort, receptivity, and the fluctuating nature of our psychological landscape.

--- Mars

Mars is the force of courage, strength, and directed action that allows us to protect and provide. It represents the warrior spirit, governing our drive, logic, and the physical energy required to overcome obstacles.

--- Mercury

Mercury represents the intellect, communication, and the capacity for discrimination and trade. It acts as the messenger, facilitating the exchange of ideas and the skillful management of technical details.

--- Jupiter

Jupiter is the teacher of the gods, representing wisdom, expansion, and the spiritual law that guides our growth. It governs our capacity for optimism, abundance, and the higher understanding of our place in the universe.

--- Venus

Venus represents love, beauty, and the refined appreciation of material and relational harmony. It governs our capacity for attraction, devotion, and the pursuit of pleasure and artistic expression.

--- Saturn

Saturn is the lord of karma, discipline, and the structural limitations imposed by time and duty. It serves as the great taskmaster, forcing us to face reality and develop maturity through perseverance and detachment.

--- Rahu

Rahu is the shadow point of intense material desire and the obsessive drive toward worldly innovation. It represents the area of life where we are driven to expand beyond traditional boundaries, often through trial and error.

--- Ketu

Ketu is the shadow point of detachment and the spiritual liberation that comes from transcending material form. It represents past-life mastery and the intuitive realization that our true home lies beyond the physical realm.
